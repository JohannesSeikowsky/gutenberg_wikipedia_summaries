God tells Moses to take a census of the Kohathites, Gershonites and Merarites between 30 and 50 years old, who are subject to service for the Tabernacle. God charges Moses and Aaron to take care not to let the Kohathites die because they go inside and witness the dismantling of the sanctuary.
