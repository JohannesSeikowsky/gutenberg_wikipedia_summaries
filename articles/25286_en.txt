Huanxi Yuanjia (traditional Chinese: 歡喜冤家; simplified Chinese: 欢喜冤家), also translated into English as Enemies Enamoured, Enemies in Love, and Lovers and Foes, is a late Ming dynasty Chinese short story collection by a writer under the pseudonym Xihu yuyin zhuren (西湖漁隱主人). 
The collection was published in the early 17th-century and features a wide variety of love stories that range from erotic to comical in twenty-four chapters.
